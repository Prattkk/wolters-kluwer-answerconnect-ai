# AI-Enabled Research & Drafting Enhancements for CCH AnswerConnect

## Overview
This repository contains a **student-built AI prototype and conceptual implementation** developed as part of the **MS in Business Analytics (BADM 550 ‚Äì Business Practicum)** at the **University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign**, in collaboration with **Wolters Kluwer**.

The project explores how **large language models (LLMs)** and workflow automation can transform the **research and drafting experience** for **tax and legal professionals** using **CCH AnswerConnect**, with a strong focus on **trust, transparency, productivity, and governance**.

---

## Problem Context
User research revealed three major gaps in the current research experience:

- **Awareness Gap** ‚Äì Advanced AI capabilities exist but are not surfaced contextually, leading to underutilization.
- **Trust Gap** ‚Äì Users hesitate to rely on AI due to limited explainability, source grounding, and citation transparency.
- **Experience Gap** ‚Äì AI search feels disconnected from natural research and drafting workflows, causing users to revert to manual processes.

As a result, professionals adapt their workflow to the tool rather than the tool adapting to how professionals work.

---

## Solution Summary
The project proposes **two high-impact, enterprise-ready AI solutions** designed to integrate seamlessly into existing workflows while maintaining human oversight and compliance.

## Architecture & Implementation
The proposed architecture follows an **enterprise-grade design**, including:
- LLM + Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Secure content APIs and metadata indexing
- Research memory and vector storage
- Workflow orchestration and routing engines
- Audit logging, access controls, and compliance layers

A phased **18‚Äì20 month roadmap** outlines prototype development, pilot rollout, and production readiness.

## Competitive Differentiation
Competitive analysis against platforms such as **Westlaw, Lexis+ AI, Harvey AI, and Casetext** showed that:
- Existing tools focus on isolated AI tasks
- No competitor offers a **unified research + drafting workspace with persistent memory**
- End-to-end workflow orchestration remains largely manual

These solutions position **CCH AnswerConnect** to deliver a differentiated, trust-first AI research ecosystem.
